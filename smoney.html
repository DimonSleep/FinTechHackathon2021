Challenge FinTech Hackathon 2021

This application is a challenge for the Demonick Studios team. It is the solution in our opinion of the general problems on which the FinTech 2021 hackathon is based. We tried to rely on a new way of creating the application for us, as we did not have any experience in the field of finance.

The advantages of the application

NFC

Is a new application with the ability to pay via NFC

Easy to use

Due to the design it is extremely comfortable to use, offering psychological security due to the friendly interface.

Possibility of transfer

The possibility to transfer from card to card the amounts of money B2B, P2P, B2P, P2B.

Foreign exchange

Includes foreign exchange, and the possibility of market research, all transfers are executed only by confirming the identity of the owner.

Security

Security is provided due to the fact that NFC technology only starts when we make payments using the application.

Cryptocurrency

In this wallet can be kept cryptocurrency and offers the possibility to sell or buy cryptocurrency.
